OfficeWebControls 2.0

OfficeWebControls is my attempt to bring the Office Ribbon and other Microsoft items such as input elements, buttons, modals, ... to the web.
In the near future, projects derived from this one will see the light such as the ASP.NET MVC Office Ribbon Creator, a way to construct an Office Ribbon through a fluent API.

Note: The OfficeWebControls have been tested succesfully on this browsers:
        - Google Chrome 35.0.1916.153 m
        - Mozilla Firefox 30.0
        - Internet Explorer 11.0.9600.17126
